# 3D models

Here are:
* the 3D models for printing (keys have been attached to the shell, for easier printing)
* the Fusion360 archive, for editing.

You will need to use a resin, MJF or similar printer, with a precision ~0.1mm or better.

Additionally, you will need:
* 14 cylindrical magnets, diam. 3mm, 2mm high ; North and South poles normal to the flat sides. Insertion direction is critical (test them with keyboard.monitor_readings() before inserting them, as they're a pain to get out).
